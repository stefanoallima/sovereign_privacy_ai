The Privacy Guard

Never Send a Secret
by Accident

Your sovereign council runs locally. When you need a more powerful AI model that would be impractical to run on your hardware, the Privacy Guard ensures your secrets are redacted before anything leaves your machine.

Local PII Detection

Your local AI automatically scans documents for names, addresses, financial data, and sensitive IDs. Everything detected stays on your device.

The Redaction Dashboard

When a council member needs a second opinion from a powerful cloud model, the app alerts you first. You get a side-by-side view to redact or approve specific data points before they ever leave your machine.

You Control the Leak

The cloud gets the context; you keep the identity. Only anonymized categorical attributes (income bracket, employment type) reach the network. Real values are filled back in locally.

Incognito Mode

Disappearing chats that leave zero trace. Messages exist only in memory — never written to disk, never persisted. Close the conversation and it's gone forever.

How It Works

The Privacy Pipeline

Your message goes through multiple privacy layers before any data reaches the cloud.

1
Your Message "I earn €62,500 and have a mortgage at Keizersgracht 42..."
↓
2
Local PII Detection Built-in Qwen3-8B identifies: name, address, BSN, salary figures
↓
3
You Review & Approve Redaction dashboard shows exactly what will be masked. You decide.
↓
4
Cloud LLM (Anonymized) Receives only: "User in 50k-75k bracket, has mortgage, Box 1"
↓
5
Re-hydration (Local) Cloud response with [INCOME] placeholders filled with your real values locally
EU-Grade Security

High-Performance,
Zero-Retention

Need a more powerful model than what runs locally? Configure any OpenAI-compatible API endpoint. We default to EU-based Nebius (Finland & France) — but you must enable Zero Data Retention (ZDR) in your Nebius account settings for maximum privacy.

ChaCha20-Poly1305

Military-grade encryption for all sensitive data stored locally. Keys in your system credential manager, not in files.

Configurable Cloud Endpoint

When you need a more powerful model, connect any OpenAI-compatible API. We default to EU-based Nebius — enable ZDR in your Nebius settings so your data is never stored.

Embedded AI Engine

Built-in Qwen3-8B runs entirely on your hardware. No separate installs, no background services, no external dependencies.

Windows Credential Store

Encryption keys stored in your OS credential manager. Not in config files, not in environment variables.
