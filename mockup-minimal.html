Can I deduct my home office if I also use it for personal things?

Yes, but only the portion used exclusively for business. The Dutch tax authority (Belastingdienst) requires that the space is used predominantly and regularly for your ZZP work. If your office is mixed-use, a proportional deduction based on floor area is allowed.

Keep records: photos of the space, floor plan measurements, and a log showing business use days helps if you're audited.
